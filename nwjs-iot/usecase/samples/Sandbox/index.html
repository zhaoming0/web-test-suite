This sample demonstrates the sandbox of iframe can be set to 'allow-scripts', 'allow-forms', 'allow-same-origin' and 'allow-modals'.

  1. There is a filled green square and no red in the frame below.

  2. There are an input form with text "Filler Text" and a button "Submit" in the frame below, and there is green text "Filler Text" after click the button.

  3. There is text "Filler Text" below.

  4. There is no modal dialog opened after click the below iframe buttons(alert, confirm and prompt).

  5. There are corresponding modal dialogs opened after click the below iframe button(alert, confirm and prompt).

This sample is designed by the sandbox attribute of iframe.
